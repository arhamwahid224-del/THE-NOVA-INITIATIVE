Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is The Nova Initiative?

It is a student-led concept based in Abu Dhabi, built to empower youth to address environmental and social challenges through community action and innovation.

Where is the initiative based?

The initiative is based in Abu Dhabi.

What are your main focus areas?

We have two main focuses: Sustainability and Innovation (community projects, campaigns) and Technology and AI (workshops, mentorship, and hackathons).

Do I need to pay to join or participate?

No. The initiative operates at zero financial cost to partner schools and students. Our activities are focused on empowerment and community action.

What are the benefits for partner schools?

Benefits include enhancing the school's reputation, aligning with UAE national priorities, encouraging student leadership, and providing material for social media and newsletters.

Do I need prior experience in tech or sustainability to join?

No. We aim to provide students with learning opportunities through workshops and collaborations. We welcome all passionate students who want to create real impact.
